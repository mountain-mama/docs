## Getting Started

Getting started is as easy as 1-2-3.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    [Sign up](https://mountainmama.ai) on our website. We'll get you set-up with a Lunch Club account. You'll start receiving daily text in 1 business day.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Order Lunch">
    Respond to the daily text for lunch delivery. Order a daily special, get your usual, or ask for something else off the [menu](https://mountainmama.ai/restaurants). Tell us how you'd like it!
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pay Online">
    Around the 1st of each month, you'll receive an invoice at the email address provided during sign up. Pay online with a credit card.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Order

Respond to our daily text and let us know what you'd like. We'll provide prompts to order a daily special. To order something else off the menu, just let us know what you'd like.

When our auto-responder recognizes an order, you'll receive an instant confirmation. Our team will respond to questions as soon as possible.

<Warning>
  We accept daily orders from 8 AM to 11 AM. Our text system will automatically notify when orders are placed after the cut-off time.
</Warning>

## Lunch Club FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Lunch Club?" defaultOpen="false">
    Lunch Club is a meal delivery service offered to select locations in Bend, Oregon.

    We make ordering easy and fast, and take pride in eliminating single-use packaging from the service.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does it work?" defaultOpen="false">
    Each weekday morning, we’ll send a short text sharing our daily specials. Reply with a single character to confirm, or send a longer text to tell us what you’d like. Your order will deliver around noon.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there fees?" defaultOpen="false">
    At this time, there no delivery or membership fees. You pay only the menu price. We’re a little crazy like that.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I pay?" defaultOpen="false">
    We'll send an invoice at the beginning of each month. The invoice will be sent to the email address used during sign-up, and includes a link to pay online.



    We don't save your credit card information.



    Since it's inception, Lunch club has been succesfully operating on the honor system with our valued customer base.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I stop and start texts?" defaultOpen="false">
    To unsubscribe from texts from Mountain Mama, reply STOP.



    We will no longer be able to send you texts - even if we tried. To receive again, text JOIN or START.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
