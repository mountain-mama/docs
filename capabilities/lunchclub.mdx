### Getting Started

Getting started is as easy as 1-2-3.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Sign up on our website. We'll get you set-up with a Lunch Club account. You'll start receiving daily text in 1 business day.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Order Lunch">
    Respond to the daily text for lunch delivery. Order a daily special, get your usual, or ask for something else off the [menu](https://mountainmama.ai/restaurants). Tell us how you'd like it!
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pay Online">
    Around the 1st of each month, you'll receive an invoice at the email address provided during sign up. Pay online with a credit card.
  </Step>
</Steps>
